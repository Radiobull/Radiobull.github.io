What is droneboi?

Droneboi is a sandbox multiplayer game, where you can build your spaceships and mine asteroids or fight against other people. its playable for ios, android and pc.

How to use it?

To use my spaceships just press the purples buttons and paste the key in to the game, the path is: play/sandbox > save button > switch to keys > "enter the key" > load from key > launch

Important info

BE AWARE ABOUT THE FACT THAT YOU CAN ACCIDENTALY MOVE PIECES OF THE SHIP WHILE PUTTING THE KEY IF YOU ARE ON A MOBILE DEVICE. don't get mad about the overall beauty of the site, like comic sans or the very bright colors of the sites. everything was made on purpose to look retro.
